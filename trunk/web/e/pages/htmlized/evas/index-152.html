void evas_pointer_ungrab(Evas e);
This function removes the implicit grab of the mouse button once it has become active. This
allows leave and enter events to proceed as per normal which they would not until the
mouse button that was presses to initiate the grab is released.
Arguments:
e
A valid Evas canvas handle.
