Ecore

What is Ecore? It is the core event abstraction layer and X abstraction layer that makes doing selections, Xdnd, general X stuff, and event loops, timeouts and idle handlers fast, optimised, and convenient. it's a separate library so anyone can make use of the work put into Ecore to make this job easy for applications.

Ecore's developers include: The Rasterman, Tom Gilbert, Burra, Chris Ross, and Term. Their contact details can be found on the team page.
