EWD

EWD is a convenience library for use with several other E libraries. Amoung its multitude of abilities to make you life easier, ewd includes managment function to hande: lists, dlists, hashes, indexes, threads and trees.

EWD's primary developers are Christopher 'smugg' Rosendahl and Nathan 'RbdPngn' Ingersoll. Their contact details can be found on the team page.
