Enlightenment DR17


The upcoming release 0.17 of Enlightenment is still under development. As stated in the State of the E-nion document that was published in March 2003, the version presently in CVS was a glorified test app, and is now deprecated -- it will not compile against present versions of the Enlightenment libraries. Development is already underway for the real E17, now that most of the EFL libraries are getting to a mature stage.

Come back to this page again to stay informed about E 0.17's development progress and current features.
